About Me

Senior researcher with 15 years of autonomous driving research, currently applying end-to-end vision-language models to real vehicles. My focus is on building driving intelligence comparable to humans—and advancing beyond it. I explore perception-to-planning with multi-stage reasoning, HD-map aware actions, and real-time inference in adverse weather/night/OOD conditions.

OurTeam — Autonomous Vehicle Fleet

RL-E2E Vehicle

Reinforcement-learning driven E2E research car, combining perception encoders with policy heads for imitation + RL. Online fine-tuning and VLM-guided reasoning for safe maneuvers.

Robo Taxi

Urban ride-hailing prototype for dense traffic. Includes HD-map routing, curb-side logic, comfort control, and night/adverse weather operation.

Robo Shuttle

Campus/park shuttle tuned for smooth, predictable motion. Emphasizes low-speed safety, precise docking, and scripted routes with language-based detours.

Seamless — Off-road / Adverse Weather

All-terrain platform for snow, rain, fog, and unpaved roads. Robustness via redundancy across LiDAR-radar-camera and self-supervised adaptation.
